## ❓ Troubleshooting

### "Microphone not working?"
1. Check browser permissions (Settings → Privacy → Microphone)
2. Try a different browser (Chrome/Edge works best)
3. Check your system isn't muting the mic

### "No response?"
1. Wait a moment - it's loading the Quran data
2. Try clicking "Clear" and asking again
3. Check your internet connection

### "Audio not playing?"
1. Check volume isn't muted
2. Make sure browser has permission to play audio
3. Try a different browser

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

### Offline Mode
- The Quran data is cached locally
- Works offline (except Claude API)
- Close the browser and reopen - still works!

### Long Responses
- Speak clearly and pause between sentences
- Keep questions to 10-15 seconds of speech

### Multiple Languages
- Edit the file: Find `recognition.lang = 'en-US'`
- Change to:
  - `'ar-SA'` for Arabic
  - `'ur-PK'` for Urdu
  - `'fr-FR'` for French

### Better Search Results
- Use 2-3 key words: "Quran patience discipline"
- Not just one word: "patience"
